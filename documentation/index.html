Privacy & Local Storage

Communicate constraints clearly: CSV imports only, no bank login, encrypted local vault.

Explainable
What this app does:
  • Imports transactions via CSV exports from banks/credit cards.
  • Stores data locally and encrypted at rest (simulated in prototype).
  • Uses AI to predict spending and suggest actions (simulated).
  • Adds transactions via receipt scan → OCR → confirmation (simulated).
What this app does NOT do:
  • No bank APIs / no account aggregation
  • No external bank login
  • No cloud storage of financial data

A Day in the Life — Why These Screens Exist

Use this narrative in your presentation while clicking through screens.

Morning (Import)

Get your latest statement in.

CSV

Alex downloads a bank statement CSV and imports it into FinSight. The mapping step helps handle different bank formats.

Lunch (Receipt)

Capture spending immediately.

OCR

Alex buys lunch and scans the receipt. OCR auto-fills merchant/date/amount, then Alex confirms before it’s added to the encrypted vault.

Afternoon (Budgets)

Stay on track.

Budgets

FinSight shows budget progress and warns if trends suggest overspending. Alex adjusts budgets based on upcoming plans.

Evening (AI Insights)

Plan ahead.

AI

The assistant predicts next month’s spending and suggests actions (e.g., reduce Dining Out budget by 10%).

Anytime (Anomalies)

Catch unusual charges.

Alerts

If a new merchant charge appears or spending spikes unexpectedly, Alex reviews anomaly alerts and marks them as “expected” or “suspicious”.
